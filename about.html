Who we are

Alumni Association of the National Defence College (AANDEC) is established as an independent non-profit making Association.
The Aim of the Association is to promote social, intellectual and professional interaction amongst members of the Alumni and between the Association and the mother institution itself for the benefit of the National Defence College, the Nigeria society at large as well as promote and protect the individual interests of members.

The structure of aANDEC

The Organs of the Alumni Association of National Defence College are be as follows: general Assembly

The Secretariat
The Zonal Chapters


There are seven zonal chapters, one for each of the six geopolitical zones and one for the federal capital of Abuja are hereby established for the association. Zonal chapters act and exist with due consultation with the NEC and carry our responsibilities similar to those of the NEC at their respective Zonal areas and constitute effective links between the Zones and the National Secretariat.
